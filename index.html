About Us

SMS Varanasi

The School of Management Sciences (SMS) is a private autonomous college in Varanasi established in 1995. Affiliated to AKTU and MGKVP, it is ranked among the top 50 B-schools in India and accredited NAAC 'A' Grade.

SMS Varanasi is considered one of the most sought-after colleges in the country for management, computer sciences, commerce, and mass communication programs — known for employment-oriented education, a holistic academic environment, and outstanding placement opportunities across Purvanchal and the entire Uttar Pradesh.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is SMS Varanasi private or government?

SMS Varanasi is a private institute that was established in 1995 and has been granted Autonomous Status by UGC and 'A' Grade by NAAC.

Is SMS Varanasi good for MBA?

School of Management Sciences Varanasi offers one of the most reputed MBA programs in North India. This year, it invited more than 50 Marketing, Finance, HR and IT companies to its campus — especially for MBA & MCA students. It stands out for providing better placement opportunities than other MBA Colleges in UP.

Is admission open at SMS College Varanasi?

Yes — admissions are currently open for various UG and PG courses at SMS Varanasi for the 2026-27 academic session.

Is SMS Varanasi UGC approved?

UGC has granted SMS the prestigious Autonomous status. The college has consistently pursued growth and excellence and is recognised as one of the top B-schools in India.
